# Implement Phase

Once you’ve looked at your data and designed your model strategy and your annotation strategy, figured out how you’re goint to do with data privacy and security and planned your user experience, then you’re ready to begin implementing your system. 

In this phase, you’ll get ready for production by running any final training and testing of your model and moving it into a scalable production environment, where you’ll be concerned with monitoring your model performance and understanding potential failure modes, among other things. I’m just calling this productionize your model, which really just means making the model that you developed in a sort of testing environment in the design phase more availabe and reliable and robust as part of your production system
