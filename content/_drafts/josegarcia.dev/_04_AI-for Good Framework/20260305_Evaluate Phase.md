# Evaluate Phase

After you have deployed your system, you will move into the evaluation phase of your project. In this phase, you’ll attempt to measure how successful your project was or continues to be and communicate results to all the relevant stakeholders.

Measure the impact of your project can be tricky, but if you’ve been following this framewor, then all the way back in explore phase, you should have defined what success looks like, at least qualitatively, or in a detailed and specific problem statement.

For any AI project, the technical component of the AI is just part of the solution. It’s one component of a much larger and potentially complicated technology or product. And so the performance of the AI model in itself may not be the most imporntat aspect of a successful outcome, and certainly not the only one.
